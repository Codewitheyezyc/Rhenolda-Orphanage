What we do

ROHM is a team of passionate individuals who are deeply committed to the well-being of children. Established with the mission to offer hope and support to those who need it most, we are committed to transforming the lives of children by ensuring they grow up in a loving and caring environment.

Our team comprises dedicated professionals and volunteers who share a passion for making a difference in the lives of children who have lost their parents or are abandoned. Our organization was founded by a group of concerned citizens who recognized the urgent need for a safe haven for orphaned and vulnerable children.

Gallery of our services

Our Services

Our goal is to provide a stable and loving home for orphaned children.
We provide a safe shelter where children can feel at home. We ensure that every child receives access to quality education. We offer counseling and psychological support to help children heal from trauma and build self-confidence. We also provide food parcels and clothes to the kids. We provide regular medical check-ups and access to healthcare services. Our healthcare program also includes mental health support.
